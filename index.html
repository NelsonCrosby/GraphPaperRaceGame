Graph Paper Race Game (GPRG) is based off of a game traditionally played on grid paper (see Wikipedia page). A track is drawn on a grid. A starting line is determined, and a number of 'vehicles' are drawn at this line. Vehicles move in turn. Each turn, a vehicle may accelerate or decelerate by -1, 0 or 1 M/s (Manhattan-units per second), on each axis independently. The goal is to be the first to the finish line (which may also be the starting line in a circuit).
